Chapter 1 Demo

After 5 long months, we are so incredibly proud to introduce our debut demo for "Silent Numbers"! Our incredible team delivered all on our goals, including incredible 2D pixel art, compelling environments, funky tunes, and a compelling story. We are so excited to share with all of you!
